Welcome to the Hallowed Hills Fire Reserve / EMS application portal. Please review all requirements before applying.

Requirements

  • Must be 17 years of age
  • Must not have any ban or punishment within the last 30 days
  • Must agree to follow all HHFR documents and SOPs
  • Must remain calm and professional during high-pressure situations

Application Process

Applications must be completed honestly and with effort. Incomplete, low-effort, or AI-generated applications may be denied.

Do not contact EMS Command or Leadership asking for status updates. If accepted, you will be contacted through Discord.

If you are not contacted within 2 weeks, assume your application was denied.
